Recycled Cardboard Shelf

This shelf is made from reused cardboard, helping reduce waste and protect the environment.

Materials Used

  • Used cardboard boxes
  • Transparent adhesive tape
  • Scissors

Wooden Recycle Shelf

This shelf is crafted from reused wood, giving old materials a new and useful life.

Materials Used

  • Recycled wooden sticks (ice cream sticks)
  • Craft knife (box cutter)
  • Hot glue sticks and a hot glue gun

Smart Cardboard Shelf

This smart shelf is made from recycled cardboard, designed to organize books and small items in a neat and creative way. It helps reuse waste materials while adding a cute and aesthetic touch to your study space.

Materials Used

  • Recycled cardboard
  • Scissors or craft knife
  • Glue or hot glue gun
  • Decorative paper (optional)

Cardboard Phone Stand

This phone stand is crafted from recycled cardboard, providing a simple and practical way to hold your phone. It is lightweight, eco-friendly, and perfect for watching videos or online learning.

Materials Used

  • Recycled cardboard
  • Scissors or craft knife
  • Glue or hot glue gun
